HO CHI MINH

The Vietnamese people deeply love independence, freedom and peace. But in the face of United States aggression they have risen up, united as one man.
Time Line
1890 Born in Nghe An, Vietnam
1911 First sojourn in France
1912 to 1913 Worked as a baker in United State of America
1913 to 1919 Worked as either a chef or dishwasher at the Drayton Court Hotel in West Ealing
1919 to 1923 Political education in France
1923 Was employed by the Comintern, studied at the Communist University of the Toilers of the East and participated in the Fifth Comintern Congress
1941 Independence movement
1954 Becoming Vietnam's President
1969 Died at the age of 79
